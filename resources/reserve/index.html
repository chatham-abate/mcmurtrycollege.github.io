Reserve a Room

McMurtry has the following rooms available for reservation:

Category 1:

  • Private Dining Room (located above West Servery)
  • Library (located above West Servery)
  • McMurtry Private Rooms (located on the first floor)
    • Room 114 & 115 (study rooms)
    • TV Lounge

Our online reservation system (directions below) allows you to book rooms under Category 1 up to 3 weeks in advance.

When booking for the first time: You will need to confirm your account and set a password in order to make a reservation. This confirmation will come in your email and will take approximately one minute to complete. Please select the 'Remember Me' option when entering your login information.

For every subsequent booking: You will automatically be logged in as a registered user. Bookings can be viewed and cancelled through your account after making your first reservation. We ask that you provide a title and brief description for the purpose of your reservation as well as contact information. Note: Failure to include this information may result in the cancellation of your reservation.

Again, thank you for choosing McMurtry College as your meeting destination and if you have questions/concerns regarding the reservation system, email mcmsecretary@gmail.com.

Category 2:

  • McMurtry Commons
  • McMurtry Quad

Booking rooms under Category 2, recurring reservations, and reservations more than 3 weeks in advance, require McMurtry Leadership approval. Please send details of these requests to the McMurtry College secretaries at mcmsecretary@gmail.com. These requests may take more time to complete so we ask for your patience while waiting for confirmation.
